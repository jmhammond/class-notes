Example 1.1.7.

  1. \(\displaystyle p\to q\)
  2. \(\displaystyle p \vee q\)
  3. \(\displaystyle q \to p\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \neg q \to \neg p \)
What is the logical negation of \(p \to q\) in simple English?
