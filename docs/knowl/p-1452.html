  1. \(\displaystyle (x+1)^2 \pmod 2 \)
  2. \(\displaystyle (x+1)^5 \pmod 5 \)
  3. \(\displaystyle (x+1)^7 \pmod 7 \)
Prove that \((x+1)^p \equiv x^p + 1 \pmod p\) for any prime \(p\text{.}\)
