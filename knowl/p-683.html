While walking up stairs you notice that you have a habit of using 3 ways of taking one step and 4 ways of taking two steps at a time. Find a recurrence relation for the number of ways to go up \(n\) steps. Solve the recurrence relation given the initial conditions of \(a_0 = 1\) and \(a_1 = 3\text{.}\)
